About Us

We are happy tails, where each tail is happy!

Welcome to Happy Tails Shelter for Dogs, a haven dedicated to providing love, care, and a second chance for our four-legged friends. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs of all breeds and backgrounds, ensuring they find the loving families they deserve.

Established in 2001, Happy Tails Shelter has grown into a trusted community resource, thanks to the unwavering support of volunteers, donors, and animal lovers. Our team is passionate about creating a safe and nurturing environment for every dog that comes through our doors. We believe every dog has a unique story and deserves a happy ending.
